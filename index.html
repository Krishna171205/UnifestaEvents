Who We Are?

Unifesta Events is your ultimate fest-partner, dedicated to taking college events to the next level. Born out of passion, experience, and a deep love for the fest culture, we specialize in organizing, managing, and elevating college fests with a blend of creativity and precision.

Our team isn't just any group of event enthusiasts—we're proud to be made up of core members and contributors from DTU's legendary Engifest team, bringing with us hands-on experience from some of the most iconic college events in DTU, NSUT, and across Delhi.

From stage setups and artist bookings to sponsorships, logistics, and out-of-the-box ideation—Unifesta has done it all, and done it well.

If you're planning a college fest and want it to stand out, be remembered, and run smoother than ever—we're the team to make it happen.
